Powers for good

On complicity through silence in tech

I got into the software industry with the intention of using my powers for good. I enjoy the privilege of having a decent head on my shoulders, and I can use it to benefit humanity.

Morally, I find it difficult to tolerate the callous remarks of profit-driven minds in the space, the dreaded tech-bro mindset. It's abhorrent, and through my silence, I become complicit.

Figure it out.
