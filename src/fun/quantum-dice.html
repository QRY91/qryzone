The Codex

How to Play

Premise

You are an observer. Each observation collapses one glyph from your dice pool into a definite state, scoring its value. Reach the target before your observations run out.

Rounds

  • I — 7 observations, target 15
  • II — 10 observations, target 22
  • III — 12 observations, target 29
  • IV+ — 15 observations, +7 target each round

Quantum Mechanics

Superposition. A glyph existing in multiple potential states. Observation forces it to collapse into one outcome at random.

Entanglement. Photon α and β are linked across the pool. If both reside in your pool, observing one summons +2 from its partner.

Boons

Pair runes during a round to unlock permanent abilities:

  • Sun's Radiance — ᛊ Sowilo + ᚠ Fehu  ⟶  All dice score +2
  • Water's Flow — ᛚ Laguz + ᚨ Ansuz  ⟶  +1 observation per round
