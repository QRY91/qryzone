The Ticket Hole

A satire of project management entropy. Every ticket you complete spawns more tickets.

How it works:

  • Click any ticket to "complete" it
  • Completing a ticket spawns 1-4 child tickets (average ~1.7)
  • Net ticket count always increases
  • Sprint velocity goes negative
  • There is no winning condition

Stats tracked:

  • Completed — Tickets you've closed
  • Spawned — New tickets created
  • Net Change — The inevitable growth
  • Velocity — Rolling average (always negative)

Inspired by every sprint planning session that made things worse.
