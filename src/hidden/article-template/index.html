Article template prototypes

Experiments pouring content into the 8x8 logo grid.

A. Frame & Pocket Visible border frames the article. Pocket breaks out of the bottom-right corner. B. 8-Row Grid Each logo row maps to a semantic article zone. Pocket appears as a sidebar. C. Pixel Border The logo itself rendered as a chunky border around the article. D. Grid Literal First literal 8x8 grid with square cells, accent yellow 1-bits. v2. Cards Dark grey 1-bits, article links in 0-bit cells only. v3. Full Grid All 64 cells populated with shuffled links. Both on/off cells clickable. v4. CSS Animations Color-based animations: breath, ripple, wave, matrix, glitch. Seizure warning. v5. anime.js Physical movement animations: breathe, wave, stagger-in, ripple, float, shuffle. v6. Containers First merged container: pocket as 2x2 block. v7. Inner Square L-shaped inner square (4x4 minus corner) + pocket. clip-path and float wrapping. v8. Quips Short-form content viewer. Pocket = next button. Edge cells = contextual links. No scroll. v9. Carousel Semantic torus cycles. Q-track chevrons cycle within theme. Pocket escapes to another layer. v10. Track White cells = visible torus track. Dark border = dispersed links. Click any track cell to navigate. Minimap Isometric layer decomposition. 5 zones, 3 functional layers, 64 cells. Hover legend to isolate.
